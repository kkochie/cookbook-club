Cookbook Club is the potluck version of a book club. It's a group of friends who cook different recipes from the same cookbook for a communal feast. It's not a competition, but a way to try new things, cook the hard recipes, make messes - all with the support of one another.

Cookbook Club ultimately is a gathering of friends sharing food, laughter, and whatever life throws our way.

Want to start your own Cookbook Club?

  1. Invite several food-loving friends to join.
  2. Pick out a regional or seasonal cookbook. Don't forget the library!
  3. Make a recipe sign-up sheet to avoid doubles.
  4. Set a date for dinner. Remember to bring take-home containers!

How many people do you need to start?

We keep the group small with 4-5 active members, but if you have the space, the more the merrier! Our gatherings usually include at least one honorary guest.

How do you choose a cookbook?

Our first "season" we hopped around the globe and visited Vietnam, India, Mexico and the comfort food of America. Now we are taking turns learning about our heritages. In our small group, that will focus on Peru, Cuba, Italy, Ireland and the UK.

How do you decide what to cook?

Basically, whoever calls the recipe first gets to make it. So far we haven't had overlaps. We use a group chat to staking claim to recipes, but bigger groups might want to use a Google spreadsheet or Facebook group to keep track.
